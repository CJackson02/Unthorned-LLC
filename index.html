About Me

Unthorned

What We Do

Unthorned LLC is a purpose-driven coaching and consulting studio empowering girls ages 13–18. We provide 1:1 coaching, personal care workshops, and values-based development programs that inspire confidence, leadership, and self-worth.

Guiding the Next Generation

Our mission is rooted in faith, community, and real connection. Whether you're a parent, educator, or student — you're welcome here.

Unburden

Release the comparison, the pressure, and the doubt. Unthorned begins by creating a safe, uplifting space for young women to breathe again.

Restore

Through heart-centered mentorship, girls rediscover their voice, identity, and confidence. They learn emotional resilience and the courage to grow.

Rise

With clarity and purpose, they rise — not as who the world says they should be, but as who God designed them to be.

Because when a girl blossoms, her whole world changes.
