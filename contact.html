About Unthorned

Unthorned, LLC is a purpose‑driven coaching and consulting studio dedicated to empowering young women (ages 13–18) to build confidence, leadership, and self‑worth. We offer personalized life‑coaching sessions, beginner‑friendly makeup and personal‑care workshops, and optional faith‑inspired development programs designed to promote emotional resilience and positive self‑image.

Whether you’re looking for one‑on‑one mentorship, group workshops, or tailored consulting, Unthorned provides a supportive, values‑based environment where every student can flourish.
